## üìπ Pro Tips for Great Video

### Visual Tips:
1. **Cursor Movement:**
   - Move cursor slowly (viewers need to follow)
   - Circle important buttons before clicking
   - Pause cursor over text (give time to read)

2. **Scrolling:**
   - Scroll slowly (smooth motion)
   - Pause at top/bottom (don't rush)

3. **Form Filling:**
   - Type at normal speed (shows it's easy)
   - Don't delete/retype (shows confidence)

4. **Zoom:**
   - Zoom in on QR code (125% browser zoom)
   - Zoom in on small text (Cmd +)

### Audio Tips:
1. **Background Noise:**
   - Record in quiet room
   - Turn off AC/fan if too loud
   - Warn family "Recording for 10 min"

2. **Voice Energy:**
   - Stand up while recording (better energy!)
   - Smile while talking (sounds friendly!)
   - Imagine talking to your friend

3. **Pacing:**
   - Speak slower than you think
   - Pause after each step
   - Let viewers "breathe"

### Script Reading:
- **Don't read word-for-word** (sounds robotic)
- **Understand the flow** (speak naturally)
- **It's OK to improvise** (stay on topic)
- **Imagine explaining to your mom** (simple language!)
