## üìù SKU NAMING PATTERN

**Format:** `[TYPE]-[GENDER]-[COLOR]-[SIZE]`

Examples:
- `TSH-MEN-WHT-M` = T-Shirt, Men's, White, M
- `JNS-WOM-BLU-30` = Jeans, Women's, Blue, 30
- `KRT-WOM-PNK-L` = Kurta, Women's, Pink, L

## üéØ BEST PRACTICES FOR CLOTHING RETAIL

1. **Always Include Sizes** in product names (not just SKU)
2. **Separate Products by Size** for accurate stock tracking
3. **Use Realistic Margins:**
   - T-Shirts: 25-30%
   - Jeans: 30-35%
   - Accessories: 35-40%
4. **MRP vs Selling Price:**
   - Keep MRP 15-20% higher than selling price
   - Gives room for discounts during sales
